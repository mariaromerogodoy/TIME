What is this?

This open project is meant to keep you questioning and doubting yourself about what time is and how time affects us. The home page is a small set of questions meant to begin fueling and warming up your brain into thinking of more questions. If you're not curious or you don't enjoy thinking and rethinking what time is and what it does to us, then use this as a way to just answer fun questions. It's up to you, the user, to choose how you would like to keep and digest the information.
